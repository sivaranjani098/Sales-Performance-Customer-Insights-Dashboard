# Sales-Performance-Customer-Insights-Dashboard

This project presents an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, customer behavior, and product profitability to support data-driven business decisions.

# Project Overview

This project focuses on

- Tracking overall sales performance

- Understanding product and category trends

- Analyzing regional sales contribution

- Identifying top customers and product demand

- Monitoring orders, quantity sold, and profitability

# Dataset

The dataset includes transactional and business-related sales data such as:

1. Order Date

2. Customer Name / Segment

3. Product Name & Category

4. Sales Amount

5. Quantity Sold

6. Profit

7. Region / Location

8. Discount

# Tools & Technologies

1. Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard design

2. Power Query – Data transformation

3. DAX – Calculated measures and KPIs

4. Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source

# Sales Performance Overview

This page gives a high-level summary of overall business performance. It shows total sales, total orders, total quantity sold, average order value, and profit. Trend charts help track how sales change over time, while category and region visuals highlight where revenue is coming from. Filters allow users to analyze performance by region, segment, team, and date.

- Displays key KPIs: Total Sales, Total Orders, Total Quantity Sold, Average Order Value, and Profit.
- Shows sales trends over time to track business growth and seasonality.
- Analyzes sales distribution by product category.
- Compares total sales and quantity sold across different regions.

# Customer & Product Summary

This page focuses on detailed insights into customer and product performance. It highlights top customers, best-selling and least-selling products, and total orders by product. Customer-level sales analysis helps identify high-value customers, while product analysis supports inventory and marketing decisions.

- Highlights top customers based on total sales contribution.
- Identifies best-selling and least-selling products.
- Shows total orders by product for demand analysis.
- Provides average revenue per customer insights.

# Conclusion

This Sales Performance Dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. It enables a clear understanding of revenue trends, product performance, regional contribution, and customer value. By identifying top-performing products, key customers, and growth patterns, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making to improve sales strategies, optimize profitability, and enhance overall business performance.
